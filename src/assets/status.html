Claim Status Guide

What each status label means and when it appears, using signals from 837, 999, 277CA, and 835.

Submission & Acknowledgments Clearinghouse / 277CA Pre-Payment Payer Queue Primary Payment Outcomes (835) Secondary / COB Flow Corrected / Void Claims Patient Responsibility Appeals & Audits Closure

Submission & Acknowledgments

StatusMeaning / When You See ItTypical Signals
837 Submitted Claim file generated and sent; awaiting acknowledgments. Outbound 837 logged; no 999/277 yet.
999 Rejected Envelope/transaction failed X12 syntax/structure edits. 999 AK9/IK5 = R (reject) or E (errors → reject).
Pending Acknowledgement 837 sent but no 999/277 received within SLA window. Time-based monitor; aging since submission.

Clearinghouse / 277CA

StatusMeaning / When You See ItTypical Signals
277 Rejected Claim received but rejected at pre-adjudication business edits. 277CA STC indicates reject (e.g., A3 with details).
277 Accepted Accepted and forwarded for adjudication. 277CA acceptance (e.g., A2).
277 Pended Claim acknowledged but put on hold (eligibility/provider setup/other checks). 277CA pending/pended STC (A6/A7-style messages).
277 Not Accepted Not accepted for processing (e.g., member/provider not on file). 277CA “not accepted” STC with reason text/codes.

Pre-Payment Payer Queue

StatusMeaning / When You See ItTypical Signals
Pending Adjudication Payer accepted; claim is in processing queue. Prior 277 accepted; no 835 yet; portal shows “in process”.
Medical Records Requested Payer asks for charts/notes pre- or post-pay. Letter/portal; sometimes X12 275 attachment request.
COB Required Other insurance info required or send to primary first. 277 remark/portal; later 835 may show CO-96/PI-109 w/ RARCs.
Repricing Sent to network/repricer for rate determination before payment. 277 narrative/portal; 835 shows network adjustments.

Primary Payment Outcomes (835)

StatusMeaning / When You See ItTypical Signals
Paid in Full Allowed amount fully paid; no remaining payer/PR balance. 835 paid ≈ allowed; minimal/zero CAS.
Underpaid Paid less than expected/contract; not a full denial. 835 paid < expected; CAS CO/PI reduce allowed.
Partially Denied Some service lines paid, some denied. 835 shows mixed SVC lines with denial CARCs.
Zero Paid $0 payment without “full denial” (e.g., deductible/capitation). 835 CLP04 = 0.00 with CAS PR-1/2/3 or CO-45, etc.
Denied Claim/line not payable. 835 CLP02 = 4, or line-level denial CAS (CO-50, PI-204).
Bundled Service bundled into another code; no additional pay. 835 CAS CO-97 (or similar) + RARC notes (NCCI).
Reversed Prior payment voided/reversed. 835 CLP02 = 22 and/or negative amounts tied to prior remit.
Overpaid / Recoup Payer recoups earlier overpayment. 835 PLB recoup (WO/L6) or negative claim-level amounts.

Secondary / COB Flow

StatusMeaning / When You See ItTypical Signals
Sent to Secondary Secondary claim created/sent (by you or auto-forwarded). Secondary 837 generated or payer forwarding flag.
Pending Secondary Secondary acknowledged; waiting adjudication. Secondary 277 accepted; no secondary 835 yet.
Secondary Paid Secondary payer issued payment. Secondary 835 with positive paid amount.
Secondary Denied Secondary refused payment (coordination/benefit issues). Secondary 835 denial CARCs.

Corrected / Void Claims

StatusMeaning / When You See ItTypical Signals
Corrected Submitted Replacement claim sent. 837 with CLM05-3 = 7 referencing original.
Corrected Accepted Replacement accepted by clearinghouse/payer. 999/277 acceptance for corrected claim.
Corrected Rejected Replacement failed edits. 999/277 rejection for corrected claim.
Void Submitted Void/cancel request sent. 837 with CLM05-3 = 8 referencing prior claim.
Void Accepted Void accepted; original claim/payment will be nullified. 999/277 accept; later 835 may show reversal if paid.

Patient Responsibility

StatusMeaning / When You See ItTypical Signals
PR Created Patient responsibility established (deductible/coinsurance/copay or non-covered). 835 CAS PR* creates PR balance in system.
PR Billed Statement sent to patient. Statement batch/job log shows dispatch.
PR Payment Plan Patient enrolled in installments. Internal AR payment-plan record.
PR Paid in Full Patient balance settled. Cash posting clears PR to zero.
Small Balance WO Residual balance written off under threshold policy. Write-off batch applied; zero remaining AR.
Collections Unpaid PR placed with collections agency. Placement record / agency export.

Appeals & Audits

StatusMeaning / When You See ItTypical Signals
Appeal Submitted Formal appeal filed against denial/underpay. Appeal record; payer case ID/confirmation.
Appeal Upheld Payer maintained original decision/denial. Decision letter/portal; no corrective 835.
Appeal Overturned Payer reversed its decision; payment expected/issued. Decision letter; corrective 835 (new pay/reissue).
Audit / Post-Payment Review Claim selected for audit (pre- or post-pay). Audit request; may lead to PLB recoup or additional pay.

Closure

StatusMeaning / When You See ItTypical Signals
Closed — Paid No remaining payer or patient balance; lifecycle complete. Net zero AR; no open tasks/follow-ups.
Closed — Denied (No Appeal) Final denial with no appeal filed (or window expired). Policy-based closure; follow-ups cleared.
Closed — Write-off Balance closed via contractual/admin write-off. Write-off entries; AR zeroed out.
Closed — Recouped Entire amount recovered by payer; cycle finished. 835 PLB/negative postings reconcile to zero.
